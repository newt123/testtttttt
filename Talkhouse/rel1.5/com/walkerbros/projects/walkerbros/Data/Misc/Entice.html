Chicago Title Corporation, parent company of Chicago Title and Trust Co. (CT&T), is a leading real estate services company that provides, through its subsidiaries, title insurance and real estate-related services which facilitate residential and commercial real estate transactions.

Chicago Title offers title insurance, escrow and closing services, property valuation, credit information, field services, flood compliance and default management, through a network of more than 300 full-service offices and 3,800 policy-issuing agents in 49 states, Puerto Rico, the Virgin Islands, Guam and Canada. The company also conducts an Illinois Land Trust operation.

CT&T's subsidiaries include, Chicago Title Flood Services Inc., Chicago Title Credit Services Inc. Chicago Title Field Services and Chicago Title - Market Intelligence Inc.
